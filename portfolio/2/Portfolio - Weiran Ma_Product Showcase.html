​A Rape Culture Prevention Solution
​

Define Problem

The idea of creating an app addressing the Rape Culture originated from the 2017 CHI Competition theme - Leveling the Playfield. Rape Culture is defined as "A complex set of beliefs that encourage male sexual aggression and supports violence against women. It is a society where violence is seen as sexy and sexuality as violent" (Buchwald, Fletcher, & Roth 2005). Realizing the gradually intensified problems of rape culture in our society, we decided to create an application that helps people raise their awareness of the concept of Rape Culture.

End Result

Our end result shows that our product is constrained with a number of limitations. For example, the limited number of participants for user research and usability testing did not yield the desired result to create accurate personas and scenarios. Also, due to time constraint, we did not create a fully functional interactive prototype, which prevented us identify further usability issues in the usability testing.
​
Therefore, we propose that further research and design is necessary. Future directions of research may be:
  • Adding a reward system may be beneficial to be integrated in the learning module function
  • Conducting more extensive user research
